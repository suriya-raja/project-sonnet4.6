⚠️ Problem Statement
India wastes 68 million tonnes of food annually — 40% at the farm level as unsold produce rots in fields, while 20 crore Indians sleep hungry. Existing food rescue apps only address household/restaurant leftovers (6% of waste), ignoring the largest source of loss. There is no unified platform that tackles food waste across the entire supply chain — from farm to fork — while ensuring nutritional adequacy for recipients.

🧩 Our Solution — Three Integrated Modules
🍛

Food Donation

Redistribute leftover food from homes, restaurants & weddings to nearby NGOs within 5km in real-time

🌾

Farm Rescue (KhetSetu)

Save unsold farm produce — AI detects crop type from photo, connects farmers directly with buyers/NGOs

📊

Nutrition Tracker

AI-powered calorie & nutrition tracking ensures rescued food meets dietary needs of recipients

💡 Novelty — What Makes NOGIRR Unique

🌾 Farm-Level Rescue (First of its kind)

No existing app tackles the 40% food waste at farms. NOGIRR's KhetSetu module directly connects farmers with unsold produce to buyers — saving food at the SOURCE.

🔗 Full Supply Chain Coverage

One unified platform covers Farm → Transport → Market → Home. Others only solve one part; we solve the ENTIRE chain end-to-end.

🤖 AI-Powered Intelligence

Crop detection from photos, smart pricing, nutrition-aware matching. AI identifies food type, estimates quantity & freshness automatically.

📊 Nutrition-Aware Distribution

Not just redistribution — we ensure rescued food fills actual nutritional gaps (protein, vitamins). Health impact, not just quantity.
